Audi Q8 e-tron

The Audi Q8 e-tron is a luxury electric SUV launched in 2023.

It features dual electric motors with quattro all-wheel drive, a range of up to 500 km (WLTP), and fast charging capabilities.

The design is sleek and modern, with a high-tech interior, advanced safety systems, and premium materials.

It competes with models like the Tesla Model X and Mercedes EQS SUV, showcasing Audi's commitment to electric mobility.
